About DeepStonex

The company, Deep Marbles, came into existence merely a few years back in 2000. In this short but eventful span, the company has grown to become one of the largest and most trusted name in the marble industry of India. Deep Marbles has witnessed a journey starting from scratch to a top-notch brand name by continuously upgrading and diversifying its offerings in terms of quality, quantity & variety.

Exports

Moving with strict adherence to the international quality standards, the company today is amongst the top manufacturers and exporter of Indian marble & granite and we are enjoying our presence in over 50 countries. Our stones are exported to USA, Canada, Mexico, Vietnam, UK, Poland, Ireland, Bulgaria, Middle East, New Zealand, Australia and many more countries
