Welcome

At Deep Stonex, Quality Is always the epicenter of whatever we do, a principle that has won us a loyal customer base from all over the world. Though it had humble beginnings that saw the birth of a superlative brand through a state-of-the-art factory at Kishangarh in Rajasthan. Deep Stonex has upgraded and diversified its offerings over the years in terms of quality, volume & variety. Appreciated for its strong work ethics, integrity, and transparency, the company has earned an image of a reliable source of natural marble & granite with optimum production capacity, world-class processing technology, a huge stockyard, and a skilled & dedicated team, and an enduring desire to surpass your expectations while catering to your every architectural and interior need.
